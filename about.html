About LumaBand

We're a group of high school students who started a company to help others and make the world better.

Introducing LumaBand!

LumaBand is a wearable device for seniors that uses sensors to detect falls and alerts their emergency contacts automatically, like family or caregivers.

It includes fall detection with sensors and can send SMS to up to 3 saved contacts.
